About Green EV

Green EV is a company focused on detecting air pollution in different environments. We provide a mobile application that helps you track and monitor the air quality wherever you go.

Our mission is to create a healthier planet by helping individuals make informed decisions about the air they breathe. With our companion app, you'll have the power to know and protect yourself from harmful air pollutants.

Features

Real-time Air Quality Monitoring

Track and monitor air quality levels in real-time.

Personalized Recommendations

Receive personalized recommendations to protect yourself from pollution.

Historical Data Analysis

Analyze historical air quality data and trends.

Community Engagement

Engage with a community of individuals interested in clean air initiatives.

Our Solution

At Green EV, we provide a comprehensive solution to combat air pollution. Our application empowers individuals to detect polluted air and make informed decisions to protect themselves and their loved ones.

With our advanced algorithms and data-driven approach, we are able to accurately measure air quality in various environments. Our solution is user-friendly and provides real-time data so that you can take immediate action.
